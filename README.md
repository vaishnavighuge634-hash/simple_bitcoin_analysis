# simple_bitcoin_analysis
## Bitcoin Trader Behavior vs Market Sentiment

### Objective
To analyze how Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear & Greed Index)
impacts trader profitability and behavior.

### Data
- Historical trader data (Hyperliquid)
- Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

### Approach
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Date standardization and merging
- Sentiment-based profitability analysis
- Visualization of trader behavior

### Key Insights
- Higher profits observed during Greed phases
- Fear periods show increased losses due to panic trading
- Market sentiment strongly influences trading decisions

### Conclusion
Sentiment-aware strategies can improve risk management
and trading outcomes.
